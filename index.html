Ratu Kidul Chatbot

A chatbot that can help in exploring the folklore surrounding Kanjeng Ratu Kidul.

Use the chatbot at the bottom right

You can ask questions surrounding:

  • Theme
  • Characters and Disposition
  • Setting
  • Plot
  • Message

Limitation:

  • Version of the story is taken from the 'Babad Tanah Jawi' and KPH Dipowinoto's version.
  • Chatbot are limited to answering questions surrounding the elements of the story, which are: theme, character, disposition, setting, plot, and message.
  • Interpretation of the story are taken from Sinta and Science Direct papers.
  • English only.
  • Can only answer 1 question at a time. Don't ask multiple subject at once. ex.: "Who is Ratu Kidul and Senapati?".
